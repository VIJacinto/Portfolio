DREAM COMPUTER

I am referring to the chassis, the peripherals are easy to get nowadays

Processor (CPU): AMD Ryzen 7 9800X3D
I just want the latest CPU, especially if its an AMD. I was thinking about Intel but to my surprise, their performance on their latest CPU models are 20% slower than than AMD models.
DDR5 RAM 32GB
I mean, 16 gigabytes is fine but if I am dealing with multithreading and a latest CPU, then I should pair it with a good RAM for volatile storage.
1TB - 10TB storage
I do not use too much space, just enough to cover files and games. I mean, the delete and download prompts are there for a reason am I right.
RTX 5090 GPU
I always have a good monitor but not a good graphics card to pair with so I think it is natural to pair the two when buyiong a gaming computer.
MSI or GIGABYTE Motherboards
Of course I would choose the latest, its the company I am surprised about, especially when MSI started making their own GPU models for NVIDIA. GIGABYTE has been the reliable motherboard for me as it is flexible for various form factors such as the size itself, DDR model choice, etc. In short its, detailed customizability.
750 - 850 Power Supply Unit
Based on past research, the higher the specs of a computer, the more wattage power it demands from its PSU.
